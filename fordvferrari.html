Ford v Ferrari
Concept Poster Fall, 2019

#design #illustration #Photoshop #Illustrator
Illustrated and designed a concept poster combining inspiration from 1950's 24 Hours of Le Mans posters from and the Ford v Ferrari movie posters. This poster was created as a final project for the FA'19 offering of "Tell the Story of What You Make", a design course at Olin College of Engineering.
