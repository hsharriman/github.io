About

Hello! I'm Hwei-Shin Harriman (pronounced "way-sheen").

I am a software engineer and illustrator with a passion for working at the intersection of technology and design. I love making beautiful things, whether that be through code, illustration, or visual design.

I was born and raised in Novato, CA. I am currently a junior at Olin College of Engineering, in Boston, MA, where I major in Software Engineering. Ever since I was little, my parents encouraged have encouraged me to pursue anything that sparked my interest.

What I Care About

At Olin, there is a strong emphasis on being aware of the impact that engineering can have on society. We learn, within our first semester, that engineers, who have the power to create both amazing and terrible things, should be expected to understand the social, ethical, and environmental impacts that their decisions will bring about. It is not enough to simply create, without consideration of the consequences.
No matter the field, designers and engineers are working together to shape our future, day-in and day-out. As an individual who is looking to join this effort, I believe in supporting causes that will build a better world, and taking on the responsibility that all engineers and designers should take on: making decisions motivated by their ability to do good, not by their convenience or potential for monetary gain.
