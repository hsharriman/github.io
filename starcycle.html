Life Cycle of Stars
Spring, 2020

#design #datavisualization #Photoshop #Illustrator #research
Designed a 40"x40" print piece visualizing the life cycle of stars for the SP'20 offering of Data Visualization and Aesthetics, a graduate level course at Parsons School of Design.
The cycle starts at the bottom left, with the stars forming within nebulae. Each ring follows the life cycle for a different classification of star through the each of the major stages, from birth, to the main sequence, to old age, and finally death.
Text along the edges of the visualization describe how each specific type of star transitions from one stage to the next, and provides examples of stars in our universe that are currently in those stages.
