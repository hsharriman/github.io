PID Control Loop
Fall, 2018

#Arduino #Mathematica #controls
Implemented a PID control loop based on derived transfer functions in Arduino. The control loop allowed an inverted pendulum robot to keep its balance within a 1ft box, as well as spin in place. Used Mathematica to derive the transfer function and plot poles.
This project was completed as part of the FA'20 offering of Quantitative Engineering Analysis at Olin College of Engineering.
