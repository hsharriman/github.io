Where are the Top YAGP Competitors
Getting Their Training? Spring, 2020

#design #datavisualization #Tableau #storytelling
Using data from Youth America Grand Prix's website, designed and created an interactive data visualization using Tableau. Created as a final project for the SP'20 offering of Data Visualization and Aesthetics, a graduate course at Parsons School of Design.
Feel free to interact with the web-embdedded version to the left, or visit Tableau Public to see the published viz.
