Hi, I'm Hwei-Shin (sounds like 'way sheen')

I am currently a 4th-year software engineering major at Olin College of Engineering. I also recently attended Parsons School of Design as a visiting student with the Communications Department.

I am passionate about exploring the intersection between technology and design and gaining a deep understanding of whatever I am working on, whether that be machine learning, autonomous vehicles, fullstack engineering, or design.

I have 2 summers of experience working as a fullstack software engineering intern with Tableau, where I worked with tools like React/Redux, TypeScript, REST APIs, Spring, PostgreSQL, Perforce, and C++ on high impact, user-facing features in Tableau's web authoring service.

Outside of my engineering endeavors, I enjoy doing ballet, playing video games, drawing, designing t-shirts, playing ultimate frisbee, and cooking.
