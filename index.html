A B O U T   M E — — — —

Hi, I'm Hwei-Shin (sounds like 'way sheen')

I am a software engineer based in the Bay Area, CA. My experience is primarily in full stack engineering, data analysis and data visualization. I am passionate about improving STEM education, particularly by improving visual communication in learning materials (check out my work on Penrose). I am always looking for interesting ways that technology and design intersect, and I care about working on impactful projects that have the power to make the world a better place.

Check out my resume for more detailed descriptions of my professional experience and qualifications, and my project gallery to learn more about some of the projects I have worked on.
